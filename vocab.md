# Vocab Master List

## Planning Notes — Block 1 (Weeks 1–4, added 2026-07-11)

Frequency-weighted selection throughout, themed per week to align with lessons and
readings: Week 1 = the high-frequency verb engine room + the nouns of the res publica;
Week 2 = deponent verbs (lesson w02-4), pronouns/determiners, and the vocabulary of
civic conflict (readings: the Gracchi → Sulla); Week 3 = verbs chosen for high-value
principal parts (feeding the week's participle lessons) + war and command (readings:
Spartacus, the pirate war); Week 4 = verbs of speech and thought (the indirect-statement
engine, lesson w04-1/2) + the abstract nouns of politics, rhetoric, and the courts
(readings: Nepos, Caesar, Cicero).

Words the placement diagnostic showed as already solid (puella, via, aqua, rex, urbs,
lex, miles, civis, bellum, amo, do, ambulo, audio, scio, fugio, pugno, dico, eo, sum...)
were deliberately skipped — no Anki slots spent on known ground. Words that slipped in
the diagnostic (capio, mitto, lego, venio, vereor-confusion) are all here, front-loaded.

Macrons are used per our style contract (vocab lists and lessons only). Example
sentences drawn from real texts are cited and have been macronized for study; readings
themselves will never carry macrons. Principal parts are given in full from Week 1
onward — Week 3's participle system is built directly out of them, so treat the 4th
principal part with respect from day one.

**Comitia** cōnsulēs novōs creant.

**Cīvitās** nostra lēgibus antīquīs stat.
* prōvincia, prōvinciae: (f., 1st decl.) - province; sphere of duty.<br>

* exercitus, exercitūs: (m., 4th decl.) - army.<br>
Cōnsul **exercitum** contrā hostēs dūcit.
* dux, ducis: (m., 3rd decl.) - leader, general, guide.<br>
Mīlitēs **ducem** suum amant.
* hostis, hostis: (m., 3rd decl., i-stem) - enemy (of the state; cf. inimīcus, a personal enemy).<br>
**Hostēs** ad portās urbis veniunt.
* socius, sociī: (m., 2nd decl.) - ally, partner.<br>
**Sociī** Rōmānīs mīlitēs dant.
* homō, hominis: (m., 3rd decl.) - human being, person, man.<br>
**Homō** sine amīcīs pauper est.
* mōs, mōris: (m., 3rd decl.) - custom, habit; pl. character, morals; mōs maiōrum = ancestral custom.<br>
**Mōrēs** maiōrum rem pūblicam servāvērunt.
* iūs, iūris: (n., 3rd decl.) - right, law, justice; in iūs = to court.<br>
Cīvis Rōmānus **iūra** sua tenet.
* officium, officiī: (n., 2nd decl.) - duty, obligation, service.<br>
**Officium** cōnsulis est rem pūblicam servāre.
* ōrdō, ōrdinis: (m., 3rd decl.) - rank, order, class; ōrdō senātōrius = the senatorial order.<br>
Equitēs **ōrdō** secundus cīvitātis sunt.
* locus, locī: (m., 2nd decl.; pl. loca, n.) - place, position, rank.<br>
Hic **locus** sacer est.
* tempus, temporis: (n., 3rd decl.) - time, season; occasion.<br>
Ō **tempora**, ō mōrēs! (Cicerō, In Catilīnam 1.2)
* pars, partis: (f., 3rd decl., i-stem) - part, share; political faction, side.<br>

* manus, manūs: (f., 4th decl.) - hand; band of men.<br>
Parva **manus** mīlitum portam tenet.
* domus, domūs: (f., 4th decl., irreg.) - house, home; domī = at home.<br>
Senātor **domum** magnam prope forum habet.
* ager, agrī: (m., 2nd decl.) - field, land, territory.<br>
**Agrī** pūblicī inter cīvēs dīviduntur.
* diēs, diēī: (m./f., 5th decl.) - day; appointed day.<br>
**Diēs** comitiōrum iam adest.
* omnis, omne: (adj., 3rd decl.) - all, every.<br>
**Omnēs** cīvēs lēgibus tenentur.
* tandem: (adv.) - at last, finally; (in questions) pray, just.<br>

* etiam: (adv.) - even, also, still.<br>
Nōn sōlum cōnsul sed **etiam** senātus hoc videt.
* enim: (conj., postpositive) - for, indeed.<br>

* tamen: (adv.) - nevertheless, still, yet.<br>

* igitur: (conj., usually postpositive) - therefore, then.<br>
Cōnsul **igitur** senātum vocāvit.
* autem: (conj., postpositive) - however, moreover, but.<br>
Populus pācem vult; senātus **autem** bellum parat.

## Week 2
