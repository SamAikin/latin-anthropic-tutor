# Lesson-w02-3 — Reference: The Passive Voice, Complete

## The two passive machines

| System | How it's built | Example | Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|
| Present system (pres., impf., fut.) | active form → -r endings | capitur | is (being) taken |
| Perfect system (perf., plupf., futpf.) | 4th principal part + esse | capta est | was taken / has been taken |

You already own the second machine (diagnostic: capta est/erat/erit). Today adds the first.

## The -r endings (present system)

| Person | Active | Passive |
|---|---|---|
| 1 sg. | -ō / -m | -or / -r |
| 2 sg. | -s | -ris |
| 3 sg. | -t | -tur |
| 1 pl. | -mus | -mur |
| 2 pl. | -tis | -minī |
| 3 pl. | -nt | -ntur |

## amō and dūcō across the present-system passive (3rd sg.)

| Tense | 1st conj. | 3rd conj. | Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|
| present | amātur | dūcitur | is loved / is led |
| imperfect | amābātur | dūcēbātur | was being loved / led |
| future | amābitur | dūcētur | will be loved / led |

Watch-outs:
- 3rd conj. 2nd sg.: present dūceris vs. future dūcēris differ only by vowel length —
  in unmacroned text, context decides. (Rare in practice; flag and move on.)
- 3rd -iō and 4th conj.: capitur/capiētur, audītur/audiētur.

## Perfect-system passive (review, now systematic)

| Tense | Form | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| perfect | capta est | was taken / has been taken |
| pluperfect | capta erat | had been taken |
| future perfect | capta erit | will have been taken |

The participle agrees with the subject: urbs capta est / urbēs captae sunt /
oppidum captum est. (Full participle system: week 3.)

## Agent and means with passives (from w01-4, now in constant use)

- Person acting: **ā/ab + ablative** — `urbs ā mīlitibus capitur`
- Thing used: **bare ablative** — `urbs ferrō capitur`

## Aspect, extended to the passive (your diagnostic insight, completed)

`urbs capiēbātur` = the taking was ongoing/incomplete (imperfect aspect);
`urbs capta est` = the taking is complete (perfective). Same distinction you
articulated for cēpērunt/dabat, now on the other voice.
