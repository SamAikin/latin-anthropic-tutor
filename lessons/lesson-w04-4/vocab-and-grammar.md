## The reading procedure, assembled

1. Verbs first: find the finite verb(s); everything organizes around them.
2. Head verb of saying/thinking? → expect acc + inf; resolve sē to the reporter.
3. Participle? → find its agreement, bracket the phrase; ablative + detached →
   absolute; translate off the menu (after/because/with).
4. sī? → sort the condition by verb silhouette (indicative / future / would /
   would-have).
5. Pronoun? → antecedent, gender/number check.
6. Hold ambiguous endings open until the sentence closes them.
